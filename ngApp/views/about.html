About the ranch

Deep Water Ranch was founded in 1997 by the Allen Family and has been a place where family and friends gathers to enjoy each other and the great outdoors!


It wasn’t always this beautiful but over the years, much, much hard work and visionary tenacity has brought it from the ravaged property that was strip mined and neglected to a restored reservoir of beauty and life. Like the song, it is a home, home on the range where the deer and the antelope play; where seldom is heard a discouraging word and the skies are not cloudy all day (except when rain is needed). There is a restoration of the land still going on with a vision for sustainable farming and ranching that respects and cares for the animals and the land. We have opened up this treasure of an experience to your family because we desire that more people could experience the joy of the presence of nature.
