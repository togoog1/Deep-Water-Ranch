COME AND SEE!

Travel past the concrete city and over the railroad tracks and come and experience the Presence of Nature! See the beautiful herds of wild deer and pet some of the tame animals. Great Blue Herons, Hawks, dove and wild turkeys all call Deep Water Ranch their home. Fish in the three-acre lake or one of our ponds if you like, then settle in by the fire after cooking outside and have a good night sleep in your sleeping bags and tents. The crickets are the noise you hear most of the time though you may hear a train whistle but you can still hear the crickets at the same time.

Where you would stay

where would they stay? You can set up your camping equipment at one of our primitive camp site.

What aminities we offer

Cleared camp site, biodegradeble dish soap and a bucket of wash water used to extinguish the fire upon exit, fire ring and grate (if there’s no burn ban), firewood supplied upon request and for market price.

What things you should bring

tent, ground cover tarp, cooler with food, drinking water, toiletries, flashlights, layers of clothing for cooler nights, mosquito repellant, close toed shoes, earplugs if the crickets are too noisy,

Safety rules

allow fires? safety rules. Fires are strictly controlled for your safety by following burn ban laws and notices and restricting fires only to a designated fire ring and timeframes so the fires are never unattended and are extinguished upon exit.
